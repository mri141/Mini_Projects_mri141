Who we are

We at fulkoli.com understand that the small things in life make the biggest impact. That is why we believe that every important occasion, event and moment should be marked by the delicate beauty of fresh flowers.
To make your life fresher and easier, fulkoli.com is an online flower delivery service operating in Dhaka, Bangladesh. As your online florist of choice, we are here to fulfill your flower requirements just through a simple click.
